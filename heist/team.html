The Great Heist

There are four teammates for this heist. You have to associate them with their respective nicknames, weekday they can work and their specialty in order to setup your team.

  1. Neither Aaron nor the the safe cracker were called “The Adventurer”
  2. Zuber works the day after Hernando and the day before “The Mountain”
  3. Either Amira specialty is safe cracking or they are known as “The Wise”
  4. The hacker is either Aaron or works on Wednesday
  5. Friday and Tuesday weren’t good days for hacking.
  6. “The Wise”’s work day was two days after the safecrackers day.
  7. “The adventurer” works the day before the electrician.
