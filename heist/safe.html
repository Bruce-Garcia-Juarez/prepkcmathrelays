Cracking the Safe

The combo for the safe can be found by completing the order of operations for the following problems. Your safe cracker was able to solve the first one. Can you solve the rest?

Example:

6 รท 2(1+2)

Step 1: 6 / 2(1+2). Simplify (1+2) first which simplifies to 3

Step 2: 6/2(3). Simplify 6/2 next which simplifies to 3

Step 3: 3(3). Simplify 3*3 next which simplifes to 9

Combo: 3, 3, 9
