The Final Challenge

Using what you have uncovered, you need to answer the three questions below to add to your report to the museum who hired you.

1. Who is the hacker?

2. Which floor has the biggest area?

3. What is the safe combo that adds up to 67

4. Using the segments calculated with the floor plan, give an estimate on how many meters the team will need to walk to get from the entrance to the safe. You do not need to use the stairs in the calculation.
