PREP-KC is requesting proctor support for our Junior Math Relays event coming up on May 1, 2021 from 8:45 to 12:00 pm.

We are looking for volunteers who feel comfortable on Zoom to proctor the event and support our students during this year’s competition. Volunteers will work with groups of no more than four students at a time for one of the three team competitions that will be happening simultaneously. We are asking that proctors report to their Zoom Link at 8:30. There will be a 15 minute break in the morning, and we will be finished in time for lunch. If you are able to join us, we would really appreciate your support!

A 60-minute proctor training will be scheduled during late April for those proctors who have not participated previously.

Link to Proctor Volunteer Information

Approximately 150 students and coaches from over 20 elementary and middle schools in the PREP-KC footprint will participate in this year's event, and will host students from schools in four of PREP-KC’s partner districts: Center, Grandview, Hickman Mills, and Allen Village Charter Schools.

The awards ceremony will be a virtual celebration on YOUTUBE Live at 1:00 pm Thursday, May 6, where we will celebrate the winners of all of the competitions.
